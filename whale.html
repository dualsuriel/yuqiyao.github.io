THE MISSING WHALE

The Missing Whale is an installation made as a 3-dimensional reflection inspired by Gregory Colbert's photography work of a whale.

Height: approximately 2.5 meters. Plastic wrap, wire mesh, black & white semi-transparent photos, thread, flash light, projector. Aug - September 2015.



Inspiration
