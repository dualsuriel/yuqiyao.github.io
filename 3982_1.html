Challenge 1: Symmetry

Draw a face of any kind, as creatively as possible.

Each time you complete an art challenge, you will gain two Experience Points!

When you complete the task, take a screenshot of your art work and upload it in the Google Form.


The number button in the black canvas below represents a type of brush, click the button to start drawing (you need to click the mouse to draw).
The button "C" is for reseting the canvas, which will remove all your drawings.
Ideally you spend less than 3 - 8 minutes on this exercise, but no more than 10 minutes.
The timer here is for keeping track of time: 10:00 minutes.
